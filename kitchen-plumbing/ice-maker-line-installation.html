The ice maker line should be connected to the nearest cold water source, such as the cold water supply under the kitchen sink or a nearby pipe in a suitable location.

Tighten all connections securely and use Teflon tape on threaded connections to prevent leaks. After installation, turn on the water and check for any drips or leaks, tightening further if necessary.

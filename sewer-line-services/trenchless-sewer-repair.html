Key Trenchless Services

Residents in Kingsville can benefit from a range of trenchless sewer repair services:

Pipe Lining

This method involves inserting a flexible tube coated with resin into the damaged pipe. It's then inflated and left to harden, creating a new pipe within the old one. This option is particularly effective for minor cracks and joint failures.

Pipe Bursting

Ideal for replacing severely damaged sewer lines, pipe bursting involves pulling a new pipe through the existing one, simultaneously fracturing the old pipe. This method is efficient for upgrading old, worn-out pipelines.

Sewer Inspection

State-of-the-art camera equipment is used to conduct thorough inspections, identifying exact problem areas without unnecessary digging. This ensures targeted and precise repairs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Trenchless sewer repair is a method of fixing or replacing underground pipes without the need for digging extensive trenches. It involves techniques like pipe bursting or pipe lining to minimize disruption to the property's surface.

Trenchless sewer repair works by either inserting a new pipe into the old one (pipe lining) or breaking the old pipe apart and laying a new one in its place (pipe bursting). Both methods require minimal digging, typically only at the entry and exit points.

The benefits of trenchless sewer repair include reduced damage to landscaping, driveways, and sidewalks, faster completion times, cost savings from less labor and restoration work, and a more environmentally friendly approach with less disruption.

Trenchless sewer repair projects are typically completed in one to three days, depending on the complexity of the project and the extent of the damage being repaired.

While the upfront cost of trenchless sewer repair can be higher than traditional methods due to the specialized equipment and expertise required, it often results in overall cost savings due to reduced labor, landscaping restoration, and time savings.
